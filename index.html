Organizational infrastructure of Dawat-e-Islami

The organizational infrastructure of Dawat-e-Islami, the religious movement of the devotees of the Holy Prophet, which spreads the call towards righteousness all over the world, has been divided into two parts. (1). Pakistan (2). Overseas The aforementioned two parts are further divided into Province/City, Division, District/Metropolitan/Zone, Tehsil/Town, U.C./Sector,Ward/Sub-sector، and zeli halqas.

Pakistan
Overseas
The organizational infrastructure of Dawat-e-Islami in Pakistan has been divided in such a way that there is a Pakistan-level executive body (Pakistan Mashawarat) under which Eight Provinces have been formed
